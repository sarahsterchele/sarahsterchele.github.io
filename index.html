Sarah Sterchele is an aspiring filmmaker currently living in Vancouver, British Columbia. A recent graduate of Eastern Michigan University, she graduated summa cum laude, at the top of her class, with highest distinction, and $15k in debt. An American citizen, she expatriated from her homeland to Canada and is biding her time until the next big project comes along.

Sarah is not just an amateur indie filmmaker with imdb.com credits- she has also worked in the big leagues with ESPN Network, is a freelance video producer, and has had a short16mm film screened at the International Independent Film Festival of Mar del Plata, Argentina. She's also put in her time as a production assistant and other labo(u)r intensive jobs.

Please look around my Web site for more information on the films and tv shows I have worked on, as well my other relevant experience and skills.
