Here you will find an annotated listing of every film and television production or project I have ever worked on from most recent to last, and my role in that project.

 

Private Editing Tutor (Aug 2006-Present)

With two and a half years of experience as a teaching assistant in University film editing courses, I now privately tutor students and individuals who are having trouble in their own film-schooling with editing, and those who are simply interested in learning how to use Final Cut Pro or Adobe Premiere.

- Can troubleshoot up to 99.98% of the problems you will encounter in Final Cut Pro!

- Numerous references and testimonials available!

Production Coordinator - Finder of Lost Children (Feb-April 2007)
Dir.: Ricardo Scipio, Line Producer: Charlotte Mountford

Under the supervision of the line producer, I ran various errands for this indie film production in Vancouver as an office Production Assistant: Shuttling cast and crew to locations, picking up and delivering meals to set, making and taking phone calls and e-mails, maintaining contact and business information sheets, and other errands.

I eventually took on the position as a Production Coordinator, which included the aforementioned duties and more, such as managing petty cash, dealing with local businesses, and basically ensuring that everyone was at the right place at the right time, and that everything ran as smoothly as possible.

Links:
http://www.finderoflostchildrenmovie.com

 
Sound Editor, Technical Consultant - Water Squatters - (Nov 2006)

A documentary on the boat live-aboard crisis in False Creek, Vancouver, British Columbia and how the government is threatening the squatters' lifestyles. The film was publicly screened at Video In Studios in Vancouver.

I assisted the director, Leon Kaplan, with the editing process and performed sound editing to sync and sweeten the audio mix. I also did the logging and capturing of his footage.

Links:
http://watersquatters.blogspot.com/
http://www.videoinstudios.com/programming_archive_2006.php
